# Prompt Design — Maison Porto Médoc

> À coller dans Claude Design, Figma AI, Canva ou tout autre outil de design IA.

---

## Prompt

Design a premium e-commerce website for a French Porto wine brand called "Porto Médoc". The aesthetic is dark luxury, inspired by high-end wine maisons from the Bordeaux region.

**Brand identity:**
- Name: PORTO MÉDOC
- Tone: elegant, artisanal, premium
- Language: French

**Color palette:**
- Background: deep espresso black #1a0a02
- Accent / gold: #c9a84c
- Cream text: #f5ecd7
- Warm background (light sections): #faf8f5
- Warm brown body text: #2c1a0e
- Muted brown: #6b4c30 and #8c6040

**Typography:**
- Serif font (Georgia or similar): for headings and body
- Uppercase tracking for nav links and badges
- Large hero headline (~52px), relaxed line-height

**Page structure (top to bottom):**

1. **Sticky navigation bar** — dark background (#1a0a02), gold logo "PORTO MÉDOC" on the left, 3 uppercase nav links on the right (Nos Portos / Histoire / Contact) in cream, hovering gold

2. **Hero section** — full-width dark gradient (deep brown to burgundy), centered layout. Small gold subtitle in uppercase with letter-spacing ("Sélection Médoc"), large serif headline in 2 lines ("L'art du Porto, / à portée de verre"), a short description in warm cream below, then 2 CTA buttons side by side: one solid gold ("Découvrir la sélection") and one outlined gold ("Nous contacter")

3. **Product grid section** — white/cream background, section title centered, 3 product cards in a row. Each card: dark gradient image area at top with a large emoji icon, then card body with a small beige badge (Ruby / Tawny / White), product name in serif, short description, price in burgundy on the left and a dark add-to-cart button on the right. Cards lift with shadow on hover.

4. **Promotional banner** — full-width dark section (#1a0a02), gold heading, cream text, gold CTA button. Promo code style.

5. **Histoire section** — white background, centered, serif title + short paragraph in warm brown, max-width 640px

6. **Contact section** — warm beige background (#f0ebe3), two-column grid: left is contact info (name, email, location, hours), right is a clean form (name, email, textarea, submit button)

7. **Footer** — near-black background, centered small text in muted brown, brand name in gold

**Design principles:**
- Lots of whitespace and breathing room
- Horizontal rhythm: 60px lateral padding on desktop
- Rounded corners: minimal (2-4px radius only)
- No gradients on cards, only flat or very subtle shadows
- Mobile: single column, nav collapses

**Mood references:** Château Pétrus, Moët & Chandon website, dark luxury wine brands. Clean but rich.
